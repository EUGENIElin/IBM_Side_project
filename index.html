Background & Personal Context

During backend training, I was constantly switching between Java, SQL, and Vue.jsâ€”areas completely new to me. While AI tools accelerated my learning, I noticed patterns in where the interactions failed:

Unclear Task Definition
Students don't know how much context AI needs. "Please explain this code" rarely leads to helpful answers.
2
Surface-Level Explanations
AI describes what the code does, but not how it works or why it matters.
3
Low Trust in AI Responses
Most learners verify results through Google, documentation, or classmates.
4
Random Tool Usage
Switching between ChatGPT, Claude, and Perplexity without clear strategy.
5
High Iteration Cost
Often takes 5â€“7 rounds to reach a truly useful explanation.
Key Insight: The challenge wasn't AI's capabilityâ€”it was how we guided AI to understand our intent.

2
Context Sandwich Structure

My go-to prompt template:
